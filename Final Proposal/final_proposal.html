My Plan: A Digital Book Review Library

1. THE CONCEPT

A Tracking Book Site

I want to build a "Digital Bookshelf." My goal is to create a responsive website that displays my book progress. I will include cover images, star ratings, and a short summary for each book also tracking progress and opinions after certain chapters of the book.

2. Wireframe

Inspiration: Zlibrary

I want it to be an improved Zlibrary site. But for book reviews, I like the simplicity of it. I also want there to be options to share your reviews if you want.

Inspiration

My Anime List

Inspired by My Anime List, in tracking series. But this series is mainly dedicated to Anime and Mangs I would like to create a more neat book reading site for diverse selections.
