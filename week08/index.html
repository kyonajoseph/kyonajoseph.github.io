NET.ART

by Isabel Hague & Kyona Joseph

Title: Everything I’ve Ever Wanted to Know

Artist: Maritime Syms

Created: 2004-2007 (When Syms was 16-19 years old)

Context: Created while Syms attended the School of the Art Institute of Chicago where she was studying art and technology. Her work focused on the “influence of media and technology on the perception and performance of race and gender”


Description: This interactive website is a simple drop-down menu filled with the artist’s Google searches from 2004-2007. When you select an option in the drop-down menu, it redirects back to the home home page that just has the title “Everything I Ever Wanted to Know” in a large black sans-serif font at the top. The piece is minimalist in structure but deeply reflecting in meaning.
