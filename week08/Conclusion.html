Conclusion

This piece really shows that art can be just a raw log of your own thoughts, showing that the process of searching/logging or just mindfulness is the art itself. It shows that art can represent a messy and human collection of all our curiosities, professional interests, and even our typos. Instead of letting algorithms define her, the artist takes her own narrative by logging her searches. It reframes the internet as a surprisingly intimate space, where the search bar acts like an external brain, and our search history becomes an unfiltered archive of who we really are.
